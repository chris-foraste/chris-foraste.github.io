Pinball Table

A fully functional pinball table created using Woodworking, CAD, and Arduino skills

Project Description:

This pinball machine was the culmination of the work I put into my honors engineering class in high school.
The woodworking and CAD-work involved here were fairly straight-forward. (You can see my final paddle design on the right.)
However, the ingenuity of this project arose with the electronics. Upon researching the various designs of pinball bumpers, we noticed that the materials necessary were far out of our budget. To accomodate this we invented a new way of detecting the ball's motion. Using photoresistors placed in small holes just beneath the surface of the board, we detected the ball's shadow as a decreased resistance, thereby triggering our Arduino-based scoring mechanism. Although it did have minor sensitivity issues, the dramatic decrease in price-point justified the change.
