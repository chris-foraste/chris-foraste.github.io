This was the first ever large scale project I undertook. Working with a partner, I developed all of the components of the system including the rubber band launcher, the wooden frame, the sensing system, and the Arduino circuit. As normal pinball switches are fairly expensive and difficult to make, I substituted in photoresistors that calibrated on the program starting to track the ball's motion. Unfortunately, I don't have any detailed documentation of particular components of the project due to having lost the files once I graduated high school.
